Introduction

Hi, my name is Pedro and I'm a professional Developer.

I was introduced to computers at a young age. Since I was around 8 years old I have had a computer at my home.

I grew around computers.

But only when I reached college I was introduced to computer programming.

Something clicked for me. But I would ignore it for most of my twenties.

Around the age of 26 I was reintroduced to computer programming in the form of a game engine.


The Unity Engine


I fondly remember relearning all the basic concepts. And the joy of understanding new concepts was amazing.

After this reunion it was all uphill.

I learned all the basics of creating a computer game. From coding the gameplay, to designing levels, creating 3D models and soundtracks.

I was obsessed with it for a while.

But now I'm here. I ended up pretty much dabbling in every corner of the coding world.

I might be considered a jack of all trades but master of none. But what I do have is love for the craft and a desire to expand my horizons.

Since I have no formal degree I might not be able to collaborate with a lot of people, but as a service I might provide affordable solutions for most problems.
